The Recipe

Ingredients

Butter: 100g

Eggs: 2x

Sugar: 150g

Vanilla sugar: 1 teaspoon

Chocolate: 200g

Flour: 200g

Food coloring

Decorations

Cupcake liners

Banana

Procedure

Preheat the oven (200 celsius)

Start with putting the butter in a bowl and mixing it until the butter looks like little spikes.

Add 2 eggs and the sugar and continue mixing until the mixture is lighter in color.

&

Add the vanilla sugar and start melting the chocolate.

&

To melt the chocolate, you are going to put in in a bowl first. Add boiling water until all of the chocolate is submerged. Let it sit and wait until the chocolate is soft (check it with a spoon). Carefully rid the bowl of the water and try not to smear the chocolate. After that, put the chocolate in the bowl immediately.


Add the flour and mix everything again.

After you're done mixing pour the mixture inside the liners and put in the oven.

Set a timer for 12 to 15 minutes and wait.

After about 15 minutes take it out of the oven and add stuff like decorations and food coloring for example.

enjoy.

Don't forget the banana.
